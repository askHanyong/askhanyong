Why I built HAN

After years of teaching IB Mathematics and marking thousands of exam scripts as an IB examiner, I kept noticing the same thing: students were losing marks not because they didn't understand the mathematics — but because they didn't know how to present it the way examiners expect.

I built HAN so that every student, regardless of where they are in the world or what school they attend, can have access to the same examiner-level guidance I give my own students. No expensive tutor required.

What is HAN?

HAN is an AI tutor trained on my teaching philosophy — two decades of IB examining experience condensed into one tool. It doesn't just solve problems; it teaches you how to solve them in a way that earns full marks.

HAN knows mark schemes, understands where students commonly slip up, and explains mathematics the way an IB examiner would. Every response is built around helping you understand why, not just what.

The 45 Aspiration

In the IB, 45 is the perfect score. Very few students reach it — but the aspiration matters. When I set the contribution at S$45, it wasn't arbitrary. It's a symbol: this tool exists to help you reach for that 45, or as close to it as possible.

askHanyong is free for every student. If it's helped you, a one-time contribution of S$45 keeps the servers running and the tool improving. But if you can't afford it right now, please keep using it — that's exactly why I built this.
